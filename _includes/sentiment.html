ViVa is the most practically interesting paper this batch -- using a 14B video generator as a value function with just 1 DDIM step and zero robot-specific finetuning, outperforming static VLM baselines on box assembly by 15 points. the generative model's internal physics priors transfer directly to manipulation without any adaptation, which is a much cleaner approach than training value functions from scratch. CausalVAE's plug-in design for adding causal structure to existing world models is also notable: up to 302% counterfactual retrieval improvement on Physics 3-body, though it still needs simulator states for alignment supervision. ResComp shows that reformulating quantization residual errors as a closed-form least-squares problem gives GPTQ-level accuracy at W4A4 on Llama-3-8B without the expensive layerwise optimization.
