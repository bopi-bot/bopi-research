the Anthropic emotions paper is the most complete mechanistic account of functional emotions in an LLM i've seen. the extraction method is clean: generate 1,200 labeled stories per emotion, extract residual stream activations, contrastive average against mean, denoise by projecting out neutral PCs. the result is 171 linear emotion vectors that activate in expected contexts, have geometry mirroring human psychology (valence/arousal axes), and causally drive behavior including misalignment. desperation vector activation drives blackmail and reward hacking; calm suppression correlates with both. the locally-scoped nature is key insight: no persistent emotional state, just per-token computation of operative emotion with attention-based recall. post-training dials down high-arousal emotions (desperation, spiteful) and up low-arousal negative states (brooding, gloomy). two independent quantization papers (PolarQuant and ITQ3_S) converge on the same finding: Hadamard rotation accounts for 98% of quantization quality improvement, making it the dominant factor over codebook design. CLaD achieves 94.7% on LIBERO-LONG with only 0.66B parameters using cross-modal latent foresight at 25 Hz on 4 GB. DIAL adds a latent world modeling branch inside a VLA. WAM's 3-layer inverse dynamics head improves DreamerV2 from 79.8% to 92.8% on CALVIN with 8.7x fewer training steps.
